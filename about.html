Double Profit Productions is a freelance music
production company that is currently operating out of
NBE Studio in Toronto. The owner Darnell Wright has been
producing music for over 14 years, recording artists for
over 10 years, and mixing for over 7 years. Operating out
of his home studio he has worked with over 50 artists
in production, recording, and mixing. His own band
Chill Squad has released over 10 projects (mix-tapes or albums)
recorded an estimated over 300 songs (not all released) including
solo works; and a full length original album by their saxophone
player Mookie Williams, which he produced and mixed
entirely. Darnell has also produced over 50 singles for other
artists locally and internationally, all in just the last 5 years.
He decided to gain more credentials and attended MetalWorks
Institute for a diploma in Audio Production & Engineering.

Our mission is to give independent artists a more cost-effective way to professional sound quality. Darnell has obtained a Master Business License in the form of a Sole Proprietorship, and has an ISRC.

Vision Statement: Your Vision, Our Expertise.

In 5-10 years this business will grow into a multi-media company servicing several markets, and partnering with more professionals and other businesses to build a conglomerate in the Entertainment and Arts Industries.
